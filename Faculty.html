FACULTY


FACULTY was a dance company I co-founded and directed from late 2014, after living in London. It was also my introduction to becoming a graphic designer and designing for real world projects. Through self teaching myself the Adobe suite, I headed the design side of our company, working on a broad range of deliverables including logo work, marketing collateral, digital and printed posters, social media content, clothing design.
