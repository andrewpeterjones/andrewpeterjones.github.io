SURFDUCK


SURFDUCK was one of my final projects during my studies. A group project that was centered around UX/UI design. The brief was to create the makings of an app for the Auckland Council that focused on the free things youth and families can do in and around Auckland. SURFDUCK is a surfers and beach lovers dream. A one stop Auckland beach database that includes info, weather, tides/wind charts and maps all with app.
