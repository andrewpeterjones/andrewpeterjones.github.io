Black Box Boutique


This street campaign was for one of Black Box Boutique’s quarterly sales held at the Grey Lynn location. After reviewing some of their older work and sticking to their brand guidelines I created the poster that was placed in 40 various CBD and Fringe locations by SHOUT Media.




They wanted certain brands to be front and center and also a neon pink (that was being used on the store’s front doors at the time) to be incorporated into the design.
