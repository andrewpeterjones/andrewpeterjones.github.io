Creative New Zealand


This brief of re-designing a NZ logo was during my studies. It had a quick turn around of 8 hours and focused on speed. Being a dancer I came across Creative NZ during my brainstorming stages. Concepts came quick as I knew their branding and background already. I decided to modernize the logo and give it a bit more strength while keeping true to them and their colour palette.
