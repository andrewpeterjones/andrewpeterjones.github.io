Black Eyewear


Black Eyewear, a leading supplier of luxury eyewear brands to NZ with the likes of - Tom Ford, Givenchy, Jimmy Choo, required a refresh to their existing identity, one which required in-depth research and understanding of who they are and what they stand for going forward. Over the past 3 years I've been the lead designer executing this change and re-launch to the market. Including a new logomark, overall design asthetic, website design. I execute all their in-house and clients marketing needs including brand launches, in-store and social media assets and visual merchandising.
