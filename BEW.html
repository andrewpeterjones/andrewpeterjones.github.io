Black Eyewear


Black Eyewear, NZ's supplier of luxury eyewear brands (Dior, Fendi, Tom Ford, Givenchy etc) required a refresh to their existing identity, no easy task, one which required in-depth research and understanding of who they are and what they stand for. Over the past 18 months I've been the lead designer executing this change and re-launch to the market, one which includes a new logomark, design asthetic, website design and all marketing needs.
