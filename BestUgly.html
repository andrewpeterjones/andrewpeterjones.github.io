Best Ugly Bagels


During my studies while learning about UX/UI design and thinking, I had a play around with an ordering system for Best Ugly Bagels, as if they were a client of mine. They currently don't offer online ordering but it can be done through UBER EATS. I kept it simple and ledgable with a touch of what I would personally like to see as a customer going through the ordering system. I liked how this one turned out, just a bit of fun really.
