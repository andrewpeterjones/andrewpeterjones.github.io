The Great Kiwi Road Trip


This project was given to me while studying. The main focus was on layout, and to be presented in the form of a magazine via InDesign. With just a few photos and limited time given, I created the skeleton of a seasonal magazine that was based around 'must-see' places while road tripping throughout New Zealand. I wanted to give it a premium look, so the feeling was as if you were gaining some top quality knowledge once purchased.
