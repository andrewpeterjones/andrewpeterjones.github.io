Michael's Secret Stuff


This project was given to me while studying. The brief was to create a product that could sit at market shelves within a fictional world. After concept stages I decided to use the fictional world from the movie ‘Space Jam’, one of my favorites as a kid. Following this, I took inspiration from a scene within the movie which lead to me finalize on a protein powder form of ‘Michael’s Secret Stuff’, in line with the brief.
