Hey, I'm Andrew

I've worked as a professional dancer both nationally and internationally for the past 10 years. After a stint in London, to then return home and co-direct a dance company, I reached a point in my life where priorities changed and I knew needed new challenges to sink my teeth into.

I decided to throw myself into Yoobee School of Design to carry on my creative lifestyle. This is where I fell in love with web design / development, graphic design and all things digital.
