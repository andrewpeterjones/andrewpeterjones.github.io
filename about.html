Hey! I'm Andrew

I've worked as a professional dancer both nationally and internationally for the past 10+ years. After a year in London, then returning home to co-direct a dance company, I reached a point where my priorities were changing and I needed new challenges to sink my teeth into.

While running the company, I taught myself the ins and outs of the Adobe suite for all our digital, print, video and clothing needs. This is where I fell in love with graphic / web design and all things digital.

I have a huge interest in the fashion world and have worked in it for 3+ years. You can almost always find me researching, designing and online shopping in my spare time.
