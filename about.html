I'm Andrew, a husband, father and designer from Auckland, New Zealand. I currently work for Sunglass Style as their Visual Marketing Coordinator - collaborating on the preparation, planning, and execution of their Marketing Programme in regard to all things visual, from design through to implementation in store. Responsible for the look of the business including the retail stores, social media and online presence.

My varied background includes work as a leading professional Dancer, Choreographer and Teacher for over ten years, working both nationally and internationally. During this time, co-directing two dance companies and working within the fashion and retail sectors for a number of years.

Originally a self taught designer, I've worked on projects covering a broad range of deliverables, which started with my dance company, FACULTY. Studying through Yoobee School of Design helped expand my knowledge and refined my skills, ideas and design thinking.
