Hey, I'm Andrew

I've worked as a professional dancer both nationally and internationally for the past 10+ years. After a stint in London, to then return home and co-direct a dance company, I reached a point in my life where priorities changed and I needed new challenges to sink my teeth into.

While running the company I had to learn the Adobe suite on the fly for all our digital, video, print and clothing needs. This is where I fell in love with graphic / web design and all things digital.

I have a huge interest and have worked in the fashion world for a while now. You can almost always find me researching, designing and online shopping in my spare time.
